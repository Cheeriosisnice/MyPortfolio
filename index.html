Creating a sphere with fur physics

I created a sphere and added hair to it and messed with the stiffness and weight. As well as air drag and physics.

Creating an Oreo

I created a sphere and cut half of it, followed by flattening and adding an image texture.

Creating an oreo

I unwrapped the image and scaled the image to fit the model in UV editing section. I then added a cylinder and duplicated the top.

Creating a soda can

I used a reference image and traced with proportional editing. Followed by adding the label and cylinder projection to fit with it.

Creating a seashell

I created a circle and added an array modifier, followed by changing the axis distance location. I then used bridge edge loops with solidify modifier and to top it off with shade smooth.
